The photos provided show some examples of work I have created while working at Morris County Golf Club. Throughout the past year I have handled all aspects of communications to the members. I use digital and print marketing to promote events happening at the Club.

I create monthly calendar postcards, junior program postcards, newsletters, magazines “Cloverleaf”, Save the Date booklets and golf outing pamphlets. The largest project I produce is an address book that includes: member information, staff directory, operation hours, rules & policies for golf, tennis, swimming; by-laws, billing & dues and Club champions.
